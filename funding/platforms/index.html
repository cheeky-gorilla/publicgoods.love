Gitcoin Grants $48,150,000 Gitcoin was the first crowdfunding platform to utilize quadratic funding in support of public goods projects. Launched in 2018, Gitcoin runs crowdfunding rounds every quarter, the most recent of which (round 15), raised $4.4M for 1,495 different public goods projects. Gitcoin's 16th quadratic funding round will commence in January 2023. Follow their blog or Twitter for updates! Quadratic Funding
Clr.fund $502,034 Clr.fund aims to be "a primary protocol by which the Ethereum protocol and ecosystem allocate funds towards the development of public goods that benefit the Ethereum Ecosystem". Launched in Oct 2020, Clr.fund ran the first non-custodial and trust-minimized quadratic funding round. Clr.fund's next round will run from Oct 11-21, and focus on public goods in Latin America! Public goods projects should apply now to be included! Quadratic Funding
Pomelo Grants $1,412,888 Pomelo is a crowdfunding platform for public goods projects building on the EOS blockchain. Pomelo was launched in Nov 2021, runs quarterly, and is powered by Gitcoin. Season 4 of Pomelo grants will run from Nov 23 - Dec 14, with a $385,699 matching pool. Public goods projects building on EOS can apply from Nov 14th. Keep an eye on their blog for application details! Quadratic Funding
